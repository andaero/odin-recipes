Fried Rice



Ingredients:
  • Cooked, chilled rice
  • Eggs
  • Carrots
  • Onions
  • Green Onions
  • Corn
  • Garlic
  • Sesame oil
  • Vegetable oil
  • Kosher salt, to taste
Steps:
  1. Add oil to the pan and cook the eggs halfway
  2. Add the rice
  3. After about another minute add in the vegetables
  4. Finally add sesame oil and green onions to garnish
  5. Enjoy!
